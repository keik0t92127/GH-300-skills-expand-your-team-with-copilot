# OctoAcme Risks and Communication

This document describes how OctoAcme manages project risks and stakeholder communication.

## Risk Management

### Risk Identification

Risks are identified throughout the project lifecycle. Anyone on the team can raise a risk by creating an issue tagged with the `risk` label or by flagging it in the weekly sync meeting.

### Risk Register

The PM maintains a risk register that captures:

- Risk description
- Likelihood (Low / Medium / High)
- Impact (Low / Medium / High)
- Risk owner
- Mitigation strategy
- Contingency plan

### Risk Review Cadence

- **Daily stand-up**: active blockers and newly discovered risks are surfaced
- **Weekly sync**: full risk register reviewed and updated
- **Milestone reviews**: risks with high likelihood or impact are escalated to stakeholders

## Communication Strategy

### Single Source of Truth

All project documentation, decisions, and status updates are stored in the GitHub repository. The project board is the authoritative view of current work status. Stakeholders are directed to the repository for the most current information.

### Communication Channels and Cadence

| Audience         | Channel                         | Frequency        | Owner |
|------------------|---------------------------------|------------------|-------|
| Development team | Daily stand-up (async or sync)  | Daily            | PM    |
| Full team        | Weekly sync meeting             | Weekly           | PM    |
| Stakeholders     | Written status update           | Weekly           | PM    |
| All stakeholders | Demo / review session           | End of each sprint | PdM |

### Stakeholder Updates

The PM sends a written stakeholder update at the end of each week covering:

- Progress since last update
- Upcoming milestones
- Risks or issues requiring stakeholder awareness
- Decisions needed from stakeholders

### Demo and Review Sessions

At the end of each sprint, the PdM facilitates a demo session where:

- Completed features are demonstrated
- Stakeholder feedback is collected
- Acceptance is confirmed or follow-up items are logged

### Escalation Path

Issues or risks that cannot be resolved at the team level are escalated by the PM to the relevant stakeholder or executive sponsor within one business day of identification.
